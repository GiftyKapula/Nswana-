WELCOME TO MWINILUNGA CONSTITUENCY

Mwinilunga constituency , a beautiful constituency in northwestern Zambia, is known for its stunning natural landscapes, warm and welcoming community, and rich cultural heritage. Mwinilunga constituency is surrounded by lush forests, rolling hills, and picturesque rivers, making it a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers. The people of Mwinilunga constituency are known for their hospitality and strong sense of community, creating an inviting atmosphere for visitors. With its unique blend of natural beauty and cultural vibrancy, Mwinilunga is a place that leaves a lasting impression on anyone fortunate enough to experience it. Mwinilunga constituency has a population of 136,770 people and the common local language used is Lunda as the Lunda speaking people are the largest ethnic group in that area. The Senior Chief in Mwinilunga constituency is His Royal Highness Chief Kanongesha.
